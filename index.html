About Me

I am a Bangladeshi forest-science professional with over five years of experience in ecology, forestry, and biodiversity. Currently, I serve as a Research Consultant at CEGIS under the Ministry of Water Resources. My work focuses on forest health monitoring, biodiversity surveys, afforestation, landscape restoration, and environmental impact assessments. I have contributed to major national and international projects in the Sundarbans, Chittagong Hill Tracts, and other ecosystems across Bangladesh.
