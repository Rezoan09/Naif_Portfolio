About Me

Forest-science professional from Bangladesh with 5+ years of experience in ecology, forestry and biodiversity. Currently working as a Research Consultant at CEGIS under the Ministry of Water Resources. Focus areas include forest health monitoring, biodiversity surveys, afforestation/reforestation and landscape restoration planning, environmental impact assessment, and ecosystem services assessment. Experienced across the Sundarbans, Chittagong Hill Tracts, wetlands and coastal regions.
